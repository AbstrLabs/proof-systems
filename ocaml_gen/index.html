Env

The environment at some point in time during the declaration of OCaml bindings. It ensures that types cannot be declared twice, and that types that are renamed and/or relocated into module are referenced correctly.

Traits

OCamlBinding

OCamlBinding is the trait implemented by types to generate their OCaml bindings. It is usually derived automatically via the OcamlGen macro, or the OCamlCustomType macro for custom types. For functions, refer to the ocaml_gen macro.

OCamlDesc

OCamlDesc is the trait implemented by types to facilitate generation of their OCaml bindings. It is usually derived automatically via the OcamlGen macro, or the OCamlCustomType macro for custom types.
